More details and rules here: https://www.100daysofcode.com/

For that, I dusted off my 7 years old twitter account, I never liked twitter, but I'll give it a chance. Another rusted and forgoten thing is my English, I will write everithing in english for getting used to it, and just in case some foreign company wants to hire me (wink wink).
